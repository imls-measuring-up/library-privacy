Our Members

DLF membership is open to any organization engaged in building or using digital libraries. We especially invite new members who believe in DLF’s mission to advance research, learning, and the public good through the creative design and wise application of digital library technologies.

Our 139 DLF member institutions contribute to community efforts through a variety of research and development, information sharing, networking, and catalytic initiatives. You need not be a member to participate in our annual DLF Forum, but we encourage you to find out more about the benefits of membership and why the following institutions invest in the Digital Library Federation.
