Announcement
Do You Have Your Researcher ORCID iD? The MSK Library Can Help You Obtain Your ID!
The MSK Library is pleased to announce that effective today (03/01/2016) our authors can access ORCID@MSK. Authors who have a Synapse’s profile will soon be receiving email notification from the Library about next steps.

An ORCID iD is a unique, permanent, and universal digital identifier for researchers which will create greater efficiencies for all involved in scholarly communication activities. This 16-digit number (expressed as a URL), serves a valuable purpose – to eliminate author name ambiguity and improve discoverability to one’s research and scholarly works. Learn more here.
