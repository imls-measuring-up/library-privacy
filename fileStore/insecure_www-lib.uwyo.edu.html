UW Digital

Fieldbooks

The University of Wyoming Libraries deposits and hosts most digital collections in Digital UW, an archival repository that serves a variety of purposes including access, discovery, secure storage, long-term preservation, and more. The content housed in Digital UW is derived from unique collections held by the University Libraries, academic departments and individuals at UW, as well as content from other sources such as community members and other state agencies in Wyoming.
