Linked Data and Art Records

The Getty Provenance Index documents the ownership and sale history of artwork as documented in various sources, reaching as far back as the 16th century. In the mid 1980’s, the Getty converted the print publication to an online database, and now, 30 years later, the system will be mapped to the CIDOC Conceptual Reference Model (CRM) and released as Linked Open Data. This talk was presented at CNI's Spring 2016 Membership Meeting, held recently in San Antonio, TX.
