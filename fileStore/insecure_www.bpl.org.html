New & Noteworthy

  • In preparation for its grand reopening, the Central Library in Copley Square will be closed to the public on Thursday, July 7, and Friday, July 8. All scheduled tours, concerts, and other programs and events will not be held during that time. For library services, please visit one of our branch locations or access our electronic resources such as e-books, streaming media, and digital magazines.
  • The Central Library Renovation will open to the public on Saturday, July 9, following a ribbon cutting with Mayor Martin J. Walsh at 10:30 a.m. The grand reopening will celebrate the completion of the second and final phase of renovations to the Johnson building constructed in 1972. Learn more about the project here.
