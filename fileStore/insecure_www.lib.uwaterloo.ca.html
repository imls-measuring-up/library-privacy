The WatCard system will be migrated to new servers, Wednesday, June 29 to Monday, July 4. Some or all WatCard services will be unavailable during the migration window.

The University of Waterloo Library invites you to participate in this month's website usability study. Earn a $10 gift card for helping us improve the Library's website.

Disruption: Davis Centre Library silent study area

Building work will take place in the Davis Centre Library starting mid-June until the end of August. Some noise and disruption may occur in the silent study area located on the east side of the Library adjacent to Ring Road.
