Our Campus

Rhodes’ Collegiate-Gothic campus sits on a 110-acre, wooded site in the heart of historic Midtown Memphis. In this beautiful, supportive environment, our students and faculty comprise a community unmatched in its dedication to lifelong learning and a life of honor.

  • Read more about Our Campus

Our City

Memphis and our connections to it are central to the Rhodes experience. Home to Fortune 500 companies such as FedEx, AutoZone and International Paper, and a major hub for research, distribution and industry, the city offers prestigious internships and research opportunities.

  • Read more about Our City

Our Traditions

As a college with more than 165 years’ worth of history, Rhodes is home to a wide range of unique attributes and traditions. And with each new generation of students that passes through our halls and joins the community, new traditions are established and embraced.

  • Read more about Our Traditions

Become Essential

Rhodes’ rigorous approach to liberal arts and sciences inspires students to discover and cultivate lifelong passions, and to make a difference in the classroom, on campus, in the city of Memphis, and throughout the world. Simply put, our mission is to prepare students to become essential in life and career, wherever those journeys take them.
