Deposit your paper as soon as it is accepted.

To be eligible for the next REF, all your articles and conference papers accepted for publication after 1st April 2016 must be deposited in an open access repository within three months of acceptance. Deposit your Author Accepted Manuscript in MyPublications. We will upload to White Rose Research Online, our open repository.

Library Help

Say Hello to Yellow!... If you need help simply ask any member of staff wearing a yellow lanyard. We'll help you find the answers to all of your library questions. Visit any of our help points, connect with us on twitter, contact your librarian or simply text or email your question.
