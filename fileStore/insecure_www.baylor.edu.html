Library News

New Libraries Exhibit Brings Out Everyday Beauty of Baylor

The latest Baylor Libraries exhibit explores the "Everyday Beauty" that surrounds the Baylor University campus.

Digital Projects Group Curator Authors New Modern History of Waco

Eric Ames, Curator of Digital Collections for the Baylor Libraries, recently released a new volume that visually presents the modern history of Waco from the 1950s to the present.

URSA Scholars Receive Library Presentation Awards

The Baylor Libraries presented certificates of achievement to several Baylor undergraduate researchers in honor of the quality of their research presentations during the university's URSA Scholars Week.

Baylor Libraries Announce Teaching Fellowship Recipients

The Baylor Libraries proudly announced the recipients of Library Teaching Fellowships for 2016.

The Baylor Libraries Welcome Two New Digital Scholarship Librarians

The Baylor Libraries proudly welcome our two new digital scholarship librarians, Megan Martinsen and Ben Johansen. They are here to work with faculty and students to use the power of digital tools to pursue new research questions in new ways.

TechPoint Enhances One Touch Video Booth in Moody Memorial Library

TechPoint recently re-designed and upgraded the capabilities of its one-button video booth located on the garden level of Moody Memorial Library. The Video Booth is available to all Baylor faculty, students and staff.
