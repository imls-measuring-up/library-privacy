The Italian Market

Craving cannoli? Everything for a spectacular meal from antipasto to dessert can be acquired in the Italian Market, the oldest and largest working outdoor market in the country. It has an old world feel to it, even as its offerings expand to include the specialties of more recent Mexican and Asian immigrants.
