Library Services

  • Power in the Libraries

    The University Libraries are continuing to push ubiquitous power sources for electronic devices in a long-range effort to enhance the study spaces we offer.

  • Instant Librarian

    Do you have a general libraries question and need a quick answer? Get instant answers on our Instant Librarian 24/7 Chat service. If you're using AOL Instant Messenger (AIM), we are available at ublibrarian10.

  • Delivery+

    All journal articles and book chapters delivered electronically. Physical items retrieved from any library and made available for pick-up at the UB libraries. This is a free service for students, faculty & staff.

Featured Databases

  • Reference Universe

    Reference Universe is a unique database that allows you to search article titles and/or back-of-the-book indexes for over 40,000 reference books (subject encyclopedias, handbooks, etc.).

  • SciFinder

    SciFinder is a major chemical, life sciences, and materials bibliographic and reference research tool. Users explore this collection by research topic, substance identifier, chemical structure, chemical reaction, or author's name.

  • Grove Art Online

    This dictionary/encyclopedia provides comprehensive coverage of all aspects of the visual arts worldwide, including painting, sculpture, graphic arts, architecture, photography, and the decorative arts.

Our Faculty & Staff

  • Ronald Gaczewski

    Manager, Preservation & Collections Processing

    Ron oversees the Libraries' Preservation Program, administers the gift program for books donated to UB Libraries and manages the bibliographic maintenance of collections.

  • Cynthia Tysick

    Head of the Educational Services Team

    Cynthia Tysick is Head of the Educational Services Team. She oversees library-related instructional services in support of UB's General Education program and faculty, students and staff across all UB schools and colleges. Cynthia is also the subject specialist liaison for Anthropology, Classics, and Communications.

  • Rose Orcutt

    Architecture and Planning Librarian

    Rose Orcutt is the Libraries' liaison for students, faculty and staff in UB's School of Architecture and Planning. Rose offers reference and research consultation services and provides support for research, teaching and learning.
