Historical Studies - Social Science Library
The library contains basic document collections, reference works and important secondary works of scholarship in most fields of history and the social sciences.

Mathematics - Natural Sciences Library
The library is located in Fuld Hall and other locations around campus. The collection focuses on research level materials in pure and applied mathematics, astrophysics, particle and mathematical physics and systems biology.

Shelby White and Leon Levy Archives Center
The Institute's Archives are housed in the Historical Studies-Social Science Library and offer many digitized resources as well. Digitized offerings include annual reports and reports related to the early computer.
