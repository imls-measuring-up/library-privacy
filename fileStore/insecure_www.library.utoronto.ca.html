Harry Potter’s World exhibit at Gerstein Library

Calling all Potterheads! From June 6 to July 16, delve into the fantastical world of Harry Potter and its roots in Renaissance magic, science, and medicine.

New venture capital and private company database CB Insights

Discover startup companies, investors and deals; track industries and geographies; build a custom dashboard with up-to-date data & analysis.

ShelfBrowse is back!

ShelfBrowse is a library service that allows users to virtually browse library holding as they appear on the actual library shelf.

Follow us on Instagram

Sharing extraordinary collections & services since 1892. Tag us to be featured.

Browzine

This bookshelf-style app allows you to browse, read, share, and save online journals directly to your iOS or Android tablet or smartphone.

Inforum branch of Toronto Seed Library now at Robarts Library, Room 1017

Come 'borrow' free vegetable and flower seeds in time for planting season. Also accepting seedy donations. All welcome.

Robarts Common

The new Robarts Common addition will be a student focused study centre that will add more than 1200 study spaces to Robarts Library. 

Construction is scheduled to begin in 2016 and is expected to be completed in 2018.

3D Printing Safety Training - MADLab
Date: Tuesday, July 5, 2016
Time: 2:10pm - 3:00pm
Location: Gerstein Library
Campus: St. George (Downtown) Campus

Learn how to safely operate the Makerbot Replicator 2 3D printers. You must complete this safety training session before you can use our 3D printers.

3D Printing Safety Training

Date: Tuesday, July 5 2:10pm - 3:00pm

Location: MADLab, Gerstein Science Information Centre, 1 Below, room B112

Presenters: Erica Lenton, Gerstein Librarian & Mike Spears, MADLab Manager

What's Covered: 

- overview of 3D Printing @ Gerstein + MADLab policies & guidelines for use

- instructions for safe & effective use of the 3D printers

- how to prepare a 3D design file for printing 

- basic design principles

Questions?

Send your questions to gerstein.3Dprinting@utoronto.ca or visit our website at: http://guides.library.utoronto.ca/3dprinting
