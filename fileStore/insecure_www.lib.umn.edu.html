From Our Collections

Performing Arts Archives

The Performing Arts Archives preserve and provide access to Minnesota's rich tradition in the performing arts. Founded in 1971, the collection includes the archives of the Guthrie Theater, the Minnesota Orchestra, Minnesota Dance Theatre, and well over a hundred other collections in music, theater, and dance.
