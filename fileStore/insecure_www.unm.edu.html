Libraries

Law Library

The UNM Law Library is located on the north campus and supports the Law School. Law Library services are also available to New Mexico residents.

Health Library and Informatics Center (HSLIC)

The HSLIC is located on the north campus and serves the Colleges of Pharmacy and Nursing as well as the School of Medicine, Biomedical Sciences Graduate Program, and Diagnostic & Therapeutic Sciences Programs.

University Libraries (UL)

University Libraries is the largest academic library system in the state and is housed in four libraries located on the central campus. Have questions? Ask a librarian!

Centennial Science & Engineering Library
sciences, engineering, mathematics, psychology,  Map & Geographic Information Center

Fine Arts & Design Library
architecture, landscape architecture, art & art history, theatre & dance, film & photography, music

Parish Memorial Library for Business & Economics
business, economics, management

Zimmerman Library
humanities, education, social sciences, government documents

Center for Southwest Research & Special Collections (CSWR)
Located in Zimmerman Library, CSWR specializes in interdisciplinary subjects relating to New Mexico, the Southwestern U.S., Mexico and Latin America, as well as rare materials from around the world.
