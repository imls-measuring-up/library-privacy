Featured Collection

"Journey" by Clare Romano. Collagraph in reds and greens. (Romano 10).

Clare Romano Collection

Clare Romano is a printmaker, educator, illustrator, painter and author. The collection represents a selection of Romanos artistic production and consists of twenty one works, including 12 collagraph prints, 7 paintings and drawings, 2 three-dimensional sculptures, and archival material.

This collection is housed in Special Collections and University Archives at Rutgers University Libraries.
