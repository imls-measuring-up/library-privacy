Library Highlights

Nobel Winner Walter Kohn Donates Papers to Library

The late physicist Walter Kohn, a UCSB professor who shared the 1998 Nobel Prize in Chemistry, has donated his papers to UCSB Library's Special Research Collections.

New Transfer Student Center Director

Meet Malaphone Phommasa, director of the new Transfer Student Center in the UCSB Library.

Longer Late Night Study for Summer

Due to student demand, hours for the Library’s Late Night Study service will be extended during Summer Sessions starting June 20.

Abandoned Oil Wells in Your Backyard?

Millions of abandoned oil and gas wells are buried under U.S. communities. Historical aerial photography, including images from the Library's Map & Imagery Laboratory, can reveal locations of former wells in developed areas.  

Music Library Open During Transition

The Music Library remains open and fully functioning in the Music Building complex as it prepares to move to a new area in the main Library. It is still on the 2nd Floor of what was once the Arts Library.

Know Your Library

Art & Architecture

UCSB Library’s Art & Architecture Collection can now be found on the 1st Floor Mountain Side.
