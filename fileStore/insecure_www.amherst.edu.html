Search Everything

What am I searching?

This tool searches the Five Colleges catalog and most of our research databases at one time (ProQuest's databases, including their current and historical newspapers, are not included).

See our search tips or try an advanced search for more options. For subject-specific database recommendations, see our Research Guides. You can also browse A-Z Databases or search for a specific journal title.

Books & Media

What am I searching?

The catalog provides information about books, e-books, films, and music held at Amherst and the Five Colleges, including the Depository. Make a request to have an item delivered by logging in. If an item is missing, we can do a search for it.

You can also use the advanced search screen or limit to DVDs/videos. To check print and e-journal holdings, use the Articles tab. To search library holdings nationwide, try WorldCat.

Reserves

What am I searching?

Find books, CDs, and DVDs for your course on reserve at at Frost, Keefe, or Morgan by searching by your instructor's last name. For e-reserves, log in to Moodle or My Amherst.

Articles & Journals

Or

What am I searching?

Looking for a specific article? Use the first search bar above.

Looking for a specific journal or newspaper? Enter the title in the second search bar to see if we have access, then click into the database that has the year you need.

Not available online? Follow the catalog link to see if we have print, or click to request a PDF through our Request Service.

Looking for articles on a particular topic? Check our Research Guides for a subject-specific database.

Looking for databases? See our A-Z Databases list.

Not sure where to start on a topic? Try Discover.

Reference

What am I searching?

This tool searches the index entries and article titles in the library's print and electronic reference collection.

Starting with these suggested subject encyclopedias, dictionaries, bibliographies, and other background works can save time and lead you to better sources as you get started on a topic.

Archives & ACDC

What am I searching?

This searches Archives & Special Collections (rare books and many of the guides for manuscript collections) and Amherst College Digital Collections (digitized art works, photographs, manuscripts, books, and more from the Archives & Special Collections, the library's Art & Architecture Collection, and the Octagon, the faculty scholarship repository).

You can also search the Five College Finding Aids for full text of guides to those collections.

Signs of the Times

Currently on exhibit in Frost Library's Mezzanine Gallery is photographer Eric Broudy's Signs of the Times, a series of portraits of homeless people from the Amherst, Hadley, and Northampton area. Signs of the Times will be on display through September 30, 2016.
